Thammasat University Life

"Life in university is filled with some of the most interesting and challenging experiences in a student's life. Learning isn't just about sitting in classrooms and listening to lectures, but it also involves meeting new friends, participating in cultural activities, joining student communities, and building good relationships with professors and academic staff. University is a place where students have the opportunity to develop themselves both academically and personally. From fostering creativity in classrooms to learning from experiences in daily life."
